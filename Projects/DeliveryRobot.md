# Campus Delivery Robot

## Project Overview

The **Campus Delivery Robot** is the first flagship robotics project of **Xpark Lab**. The goal is to design and develop an autonomous mobile robot capable of transporting small items such as documents, books, food, medicines, and parcels within a university campus. The robot will navigate safely using sensors and AI-based technologies while minimizing human intervention.

This project serves as a foundation for future autonomous robots that can be deployed in universities, hospitals, offices, warehouses, factories, and smart cities.

---

# Problem Statement

Universities and educational institutions frequently require the transportation of small items between classrooms, laboratories, libraries, offices, and cafeterias. Currently, these deliveries depend on students or staff, resulting in unnecessary delays, increased workload, and inefficient use of human resources.

An autonomous delivery robot can solve this problem by providing a reliable, safe, and efficient delivery system that operates independently inside the campus.
